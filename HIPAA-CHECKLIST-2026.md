# HIPAA Compliance Checklist (2026)

_Not legal advice. Use for organizational risk management and compliance planning._

## 1. Administrative Safeguards
- Appoint a HIPAA compliance officer (annual training required)
- Conduct annual risk assessments and document actions
- Implement written policies: access, use, breach, contingency, sanctions
- Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) required for any vendor with PHI access
- Monitor and audit PHI access

## 2. Physical Safeguards
- Secure physical premises and entry
- Device/workstation security (auto-lock, secure disposal, access tracking)

## 3. Technical Safeguards
- Unique user IDs, role-based access, MFA
- Enable audit logging, integrity protections
- Encrypt PHI in transit
- Require authentication for ePHI

## 4. Privacy Rule
- Patient Notice of Privacy Practices
- Rights management and timely response
- Minimum necessary use/disclosure

## 5. Breach Notification Rule
- Document/test a breach response plan
- Notify parties + HHS if needed (60 days max)
- Annual breach log/report if applicable

## 6. Ongoing Maintenance
- Annual audits & policy updates
- Vendor/business associate audits

*See HHS.gov and qualified counsel for specifics or legal requirements.*
